# The Yellow Guardian of the Sea | Fine Art Print

**Artist:** Luigi Andriulo  
**Series:** Coastal Boundaries & Geometries  
**Location:** Italy (Mediterranean Coast)

## Artwork Concept
"The Yellow Guardian of the Sea" explores the concept of the "border" through color and repetition. The weathered texture of the wooden fence serves as a rhythmic foreground that slices the landscape into vertical segments, contrasting with the vast, fluid horizontal of the sea. The presence of sailboats on the horizon adds a layer of depth and narrative to the geometric stillness.

## Curator & Designer Notes
- **Visual Style:** Color-Block Minimalism / Rhythmic Composition.
- **Color Palette:** Primary Yellow, Navy Blue, Sand Beige, Cloud White.
- **Best Placement:** Large scale prints for airy, modern interiors or maritime-themed professional environments.
