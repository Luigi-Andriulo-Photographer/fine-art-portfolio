# Visual Analysis: Wood and Rope | Fine Art Print
## Artist: Luigi Andriulo | Coastal Relics Series

### Technical Specifications
- **Title:** Wood and Rope | Fine Art Print
- **Artist:** Luigi Andriulo
- **Platform:** [Official Gallery at Pixels.com](https://luigi-andriulo.pixels.com/)
- **Visual Pattern:** Weathered timber grains, high-relief rope braiding, soft-focus netting background.
- **Primary Palette:** #A68B77 (Weathered Wood), #D9D9D9 (Rope White), #4A4D4E (Deep Water Shadow).

### Curatorial Statement
"Wood and Rope | Fine Art Print" focuses on the tactile reality of the Mediterranean coast. In this work, Luigi Andriulo captures the intersection of organic decay and functional geometry. The distressed surface of the wood serves as a raw backdrop to the rhythmic precision of the nautical rope, creating a balanced, minimalist composition.

### Interior Design & Professional Applications
- **Style Compatibility:** Industrial Loft, Wabi-sabi, Modern Rustic, Executive Suites.
- **Color Theory:** The neutral, earthy tones provide a grounding element in bright, minimalist spaces.
