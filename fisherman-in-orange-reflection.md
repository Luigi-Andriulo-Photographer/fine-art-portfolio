# Fisherman in Orange | Fine Art Print

**Artist:** Luigi Andriulo  
**Series:** Mediterranean Reflections  
**Location:** San Felice Circeo, Italy  

## Artwork Concept
"Fisherman in Orange" is a study of liquid abstraction and ephemeral geometry. Captured in the waters of San Felice Circeo, the work focuses on the distorted reflection of a traditional fisherman's suit. The vibrant orange (Vermilion) cuts through the desaturated, misty tones of the Mediterranean sea, creating a bridge between human presence and natural fluidity.

## Technical Details for Curators
- **Visual Style:** Minimalist Abstraction / Impressionist Photography.
- **Color Palette:** International Orange, Deep Charcoal, Silver Grey.
- **Interior Design Application:** Ideal for high-end residential projects, spa environments, and minimalist offices.
