# Floral Hand | Fine Art Print

**Artist:** Luigi Andriulo  
**Series:** Botanical Silence  
**Theme:** Anthropomorphic Nature & Soft Contrast

## Artwork Concept
"Floral Hand" explores the intersection between botanical forms and human gestures. The macro perspective emphasizes the unique structure of the Dimorphotheca petals, which emerge from the center like slender fingers reaching for the light. This work is a study in "Biophilic Minimalism," where the organic subject is isolated to highlight its architectural grace and calming color palette.

## Technical Details for Designers
- **Visual Style:** Macro Photography / High-End Botanical.
- **Color Palette:** Lavender, Cobalt Blue, Sage Green, Muted Grey.
- **Interior Application:** Designed for spaces that promote mindfulness, such as contemporary living areas, bedroom sanctuaries, or high-end spa environments.
