# Chromasensory | Fine Art Print

**Artist:** Luigi Andriulo  
**Series:** Botanical Minimalism  
**Theme:** Mediterranean Nature & Color Theory  

## Artwork Concept
"Chromasensory" is an exploration of the micro-geometries found in coastal flora. The work focuses on the intense chromatic contrast between the warm tones of the botanical elements (vibrant orange and lime green) and the cool, desaturated bokeh of the sea. It represents the "silent life" of the Mediterranean coast, following a Zen aesthetic of simplicity and focus.

## Interior Design Context
- **Style:** Organic Modern, Japandi, Minimalist.
- **Color Palette:** Citrus Green, Bright Orange, Cerulean Blue.
- **Room Placement:** Ideal for wellness spaces, modern living rooms, or professional studios seeking a calming influence.
