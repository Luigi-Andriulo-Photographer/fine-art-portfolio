# Red Suggestion Deep Inside | Fine Art Print

**Artist:** Luigi Andriulo  
**Series:** Mediterranean Reflections  
**Theme:** Chromatic Contrast & Fluidity

## Artwork Concept
"Red Suggestion Deep Inside" is a study on the distortion of primary forms through the medium of water. The work captures the vibrant red of a lifebuoy as it is broken down into fluid, dreamlike shapes by the dark teal surface of the sea. It explores the tension between safety (the buoy) and the mysterious depths of the subconscious, represented by the liquid abstraction.

- **Visual Style:** Bold Abstract / Emotional Minimalism.
- **Color Palette:** Deep Crimson, Dark Teal, Slate Grey.
