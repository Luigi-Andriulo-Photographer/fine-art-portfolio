# Tide's Offering | Fine Art Print

**Artist:** Luigi Andriulo  
**Series:** Coastal Fragments & Zen Patterns  
**Theme:** Organic Minimalism / Wabi-sabi

## Artwork Concept
"Tide's Offering" is a contemplative study on the act of collecting and organizing natural elements. By placing sea shells in a deliberate grid-like arrangement on weathered driftwood, the work creates a dialogue between the chaotic forces of the ocean and the human desire for order. It is an invitation to find beauty in the small, repetitive details of the Mediterranean coast.

## Visual Narrative for Designers
- **Visual Style:** Textural, Rhythmic, Organic.
- **Color Palette:** Terracotta, Sandy Beige, Weathered Grey, Shell Pink.
- **Ideal Placement:** Excellent for wellness centers, spa environments, or minimalist coastal homes where natural textures are a priority.
