# Visual Analysis: Summer Vibes | Fine Art Print
## Artist: Luigi Andriulo | Mediterranean Fragments Series

### Technical Specifications
- **Title:** Summer Vibes | Fine Art Print
- **Artist:** Luigi Andriulo
- **Official Gallery:** [View on Pixels.com](https://luigi-andriulo.pixels.com/)
- **Visual Pattern:** Weathered wood textures, hand-painted typography, pastel color blocking.
- **Primary Palette:** #97CBB9 (Mint), #8FB9E2 (Soft Blue), #D94551 (Heart Red).

### Curatorial Statement
"Summer Vibes" explores the intersection of human signage and natural erosion. Part of the "Mediterranean Fragments" series, this work captures the nostalgic essence of coastal life through the lens of fragile geometry. The distressed textures of the wood serve as a canvas for the vibrant colors of the Italian summer.

### Interior Design & Professional Applications
- **Style Compatibility:** Coastal Modern, Shabby Chic, Kids' Rooms, Vacation Rentals.
- **Color Theory:** The mint and blue tones provide a calming backdrop, while the red heart offers a focal point of warmth and energy.
