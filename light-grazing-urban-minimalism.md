# Light grazing in my hair | Fine Art Print

**Artist:** Luigi Andriulo  
**Series:** Urban Fragments & Human Scale  
**Location:** Italy (Harbor Scene)

## Artwork Concept
"Light grazing in my hair" is a study of monumentalism and personal reflection. The photograph captures the stark contrast between the immense, cold steel of a naval vessel (F 597) and the warm, delicate detail of sunlight catching a person's hair. It explores the feeling of "waiting" and the silence found in busy industrial environments.

## Visual Narrative for Designers
- **Composition:** Asymmetric, focusing on scale and light textures.
- **Color Palette:** Steel Grey, Warm Golden Sunlight, Terracotta accents.
- **Interior Application:** Perfect for spaces that value architectural depth and storytelling, such as modern lofts or creative studios.
