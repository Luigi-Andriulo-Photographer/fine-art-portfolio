# Floral Prudence | Fine Art Print

**Artist:** Luigi Andriulo  
**Series:** Botanical Silence  
**Theme:** Soft Minimalism & Biophilic Grace

## Artwork Concept
"Floral Prudence" is a study in botanical vulnerability and elegance. The composition focuses on the intentional tilt of the flower head, suggesting a sense of "shyness" or "prudence" within the natural world. The use of a wide aperture creates a deep bokeh that isolates the subject, transforming a simple wildflower into a sophisticated symbol of quiet luxury.

## Visual Narrative for Designers
- **Visual Style:** Soft Focus Macro / Scandinavian Minimalist.
- **Color Palette:** Lavender Purple, Sage Green, Dusty Grey, Pale White.
- **Ideal Placement:** Perfect for wellness spaces, modern nurseries, or minimalist living rooms where a calming organic focal point is needed.
