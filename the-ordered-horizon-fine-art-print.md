# Visual Analysis: The Ordered Horizon | Fine Art Print
## Artist: Luigi Andriulo | Mediterranean Fragments Series

### Technical Specifications
- **Title:** The Ordered Horizon | Fine Art Print
- **Location:** Porto di San Felice Circeo, Italy
- **Visual Pattern:** Architectural framing, rhythmic placement of nautical elements, foreground depth.
- **Primary Palette:** #5D85B3 (Sky Blue), #A1B8D1 (Pale Azure), #232323 (Shadow Black).

### Curatorial Statement
In "The Ordered Horizon," Luigi Andriulo explores the concept of "contained freedom." By utilizing a foreground railing and wall as a visual grid, the artist imposes a fragile geometry upon the maritime landscape of San Felice Circeo, transforming a regatta into a rhythmic, abstract study of space and blue tones.

### Interior Design & Professional Applications
- **Style Compatibility:** Coastal Modern, Executive Offices, Minimalist Residential.
- **Placement Tip:** Works best as a standalone large-format print to emphasize the depth created by the architectural framing.
